# Turkey Gumbo

## Ingredients

### Gumbo

- [ ] 3/4 cup vegetable oil
- [ ] 3/4 cup flour
- [ ] 2 cups chopped onions
- [ ] 1/2 cup chopped bell peppers
- [ ] 1/2 cup chopped celery
- [ ] 1 teaspoon salt
- [ ] 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
- [ ] 1/2 pound smoked sausage (such as andouille or kielbasa), chopped
- [ ] 3 quarts turkey broth (or chicken broth)
- [ ] Reserved cooked turkey meat (about 2 cups, shredded or chopped)
- [ ] 2 tablespoons chopped parsley
- [ ] 2 tablespoons chopped green onion

### For Serving

- [ ] Steamed white rice

## Directions

1. [ ] **Make the Roux:** Combine the **vegetable oil** and **flour** in a heavy-bottomed cast-iron pot or Dutch oven. Cook over medium-low heat, stirring slowly and consistently for 20 to 25 minutes, to create a dark brown roux (the color of chocolate).
2. [ ] **Saut√© the Vegetables:** Season the **onions**, **bell peppers**, and **celery** with **salt** and **cayenne pepper**. Add them to the roux. Cook and stir over medium heat until the vegetables are soft, about 5 minutes.
3. [ ] **Add Sausage and Broth:** Add the **sausage** and cook, stirring often, for 5 to 7 minutes. Add the **turkey broth** and bring the mixture to a boil.
4. [ ] **Simmer:** Reduce the heat to medium-low and simmer, uncovered, for 45 minutes.
5. [ ] **Add Turkey:** Add the **reserved cooked turkey meat** and simmer for an additional 30 minutes.
6. [ ] **Finish:** Reduce the heat to low. Stir in the **parsley** and **green onions**. Cook for about 5 minutes more. Taste for seasoning and adjust the salt or cayenne as needed.
7. [ ] **Serve:** Ladle the gumbo into bowls and top with a scoop of **steamed white rice**.
